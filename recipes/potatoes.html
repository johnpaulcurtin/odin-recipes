JPC's Potatoes

Description

A simple, delicious recipe for potatoes with a variety of interchangeable vegetables. Works very well with add fried eggs and/or bacon

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • peppers
  • onions
  • garlic
  • vegetables
  • spices

Steps

  1. rinse and prep all ingredients
  2. in a preheated skillet with oil at medium heat add potatoes with salt
  3. once potatoes are slightly browned on one side flip to other
  4. once potatoes are browned on both sides add peppers and occasionally stir
  5. after peppers have simmered for a bit add onions and spices
  6. allow onions to cook briefly and add garlic
  7. once garlic has begun to cook add your choice of vegetables
  8. once vegetables have properly cooked add more spices mix all together and serve
  9. enjoy!
