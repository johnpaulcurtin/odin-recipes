JPC's Broccolini

Description

A simple healthy side that works well alone or alongside an entree such as JPC's Potatoes or a side such as JPC's Tomatoes or both!

Ingredients

  • broccolini
  • fresh lemon juice
  • chopped garlic
  • herb/spices

Ingredients

  1. rinse and prep broccolini
  2. in a skillet over medium heat with olive oil add garlic
  3. as garlic browns add broccoline with herbs/spices
  4. allow to cook briefly and stir while adding lemon juice
  5. add more herbs/spices
  6. once properly cooked - serve
  7. enjoy!
