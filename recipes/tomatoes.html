JPC's Tomatoes

Description

A quick and easy side that works alone as a snack or with an entree such as JPC's Potatoes.

Ingredients

  • cherry tomatoes
  • spices/herbs
  • balsamic vinegar
  • cheese (optional/of your choosing)

    • Steps

    1. rinse tomatoes
    2. add tomatoes to a preheated skillet with olive oil over medium heat
    3. as tomatoes simmer add spices/herbs
    4. as tomatoes begin to blister carefully add a few splashes of balsamic vineger to taste
    5. remove from heat add more spices/herbs and serve
    6. enjoy!
